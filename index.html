A little about us

New Forest Executive Travel offer an unsurpassed professional chauffeur service to business clients and discerning individuals. Our smart, polite and punctual drivers will ensure you reach your destination on time and in comfort, and they are on hand to offer any assistance that will make your journey more enjoyable.

Formed in 1996 as Unit drivers in the film industry at Shepperton & Pinewood Film Studios, New Forest Executive Travel, are now based in the coastal town of Hythe in the New Forest, Hampshire. New Forest Executive Travel are a true Chauffeur company providing an old fashioned, first class service to both private and business clients.

Our drivers

Our Chauffeurs are trained to the highest standards in the world as implemented by Rolls Royce and the Institute of Advanced Motorists.

We pride ourselves on always being punctual, polite and smartly dressed. Our aim is to ensure that your journey is safe, secure and comfortable at all times no matter how bad the weather is or how difficult the road conditions may be.

Our drivers possess a vast knowledge of airports and ports and are on hand to help should you or your clients require assistance at arrivals or departures when travelling.

All of our chauffeurs are fully insured and licensed by the local authority.

What we do

We work 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We will ensure that you reach your destination in comfort, style and most importantly on time!

We offer a "meet and greet" service at all major airports and ports and our service includes newspapers, magazines and mineral water for the journey.
